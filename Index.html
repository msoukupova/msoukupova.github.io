About

I am a PhD student at the Ecole Normale Superieure. There I work in Stefano Palminteri’s team on a project that investigates the role of context in value-based learning and memory. In particular, we investigate why people behave suboptimally when they encounter known options in new contexts. I have a Master’s degree in Pharmacology (University of Glasgow) and Neuroscience (Freie Universitaet Berlin) and mostly study human decision making and its limitations. My hope is that one day I will figure out the Ultimate Question of Life, the Universe, and Everything.
